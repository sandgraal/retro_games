Privacy & Data FAQ

Your collection, your rules.

Dragon's Hoard Atlas is built to keep your retro collection close at hand and under your control. Here's how we handle data today.

Local-first by default

Everything you track (statuses, notes, preferences) lives in your browser's storage. No account is required and your collection stays on your device until you decide to move it.

Optional Supabase metadata

If you enable Supabase, we only use it for catalog data, price metadata, and sign-in so you can sync with community updates. Your personal statuses and notes are still stored locally and aren't sent to Supabase.

Turn Supabase off at any time from Settings. You'll keep your local library intact.

Works offline

The built-in service worker caches the app shell so you can browse and update your collection on a plane or spotty Wi-Fi. Changes you make while offline stay local and persist between sessions.

Backups, exports, and sharing

  • Create a backup JSON file whenever you like.
  • Restore from a backup to move devices or roll back.
  • Export and share snapshots with friends without creating an account.

You control when and where copies live—everything starts on your device.
